Ingredients 2 sliced chicken breast
2 cups chopped romaine lettuce
1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
8 slices of bread
1 tomato sliced
Instructions Preapre the chicken: Slice the chicken breast into thin strips and season with salt and pepper. Grill or pan-fry until cooked through.
Assemble the sandwich: Toast the bread slices. On one slice, layer the grilled chicken, lettuce, tomato, and Parmesan cheese. Top with another slice of bread. Repeat for the second sandwich.
200 cal
Carbs: 10 g
Protein: 50 g
Fats: 5 g
Prep: 15 mins
